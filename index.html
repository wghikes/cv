Design & Development

At a young age, I was instinctively curious how things worked. Much to my mother's dismay, I'd dissasemble items around the house attempting to understand how they worked and then reassemble them. This grew into a natural gravitation to computers and the technologies that made them run. My father was truely amazed one night when, after three hours, a technician couldn't get a memory module to work in his $3000 workstation but after he left, I got it working in 20 mins. This was the early 80's when things weren't plug and play. It was then that I knew computer and coding was the thing for me. I had a natural talent to understand how computers and its technology operated and I had a desire to make them run better. Over time, I have gained a wealth of information into all aspects of technology and have used that wisdom wisely. Web Development is just one important passion of mine.
